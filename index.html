About Us

The Commission

The Cross River State Road Maintenance Agency (CRSRMA) is a governmental body responsible for the maintenance, rehabilitation, and preservation of road infrastructure throughout Cross River State. Its primary goal is to ensure that the state's roads remain safe, durable, and accessible for all users, promoting efficient transportation, commerce, and connectivity across urban and rural areas. The agency also plays a key role in monitoring road conditions, responding to emergency repairs, and implementing sustainable road management practices.

Dedication

With unwavering commitment, we tirelessly work to ensure the integrity and sustainability of our state's road infrastructure, driven by a passion to serve our community.

Service

Key projects

CRSRMA is dedicated to the continuous improvement and maintenance of Cross River State’s road infrastructure through several critical initiatives. These projects are designed to enhance road safety, extend the lifespan of key transportation routes, and promote seamless mobility for both urban and rural areas. Our efforts are driven by a commitment to sustainability, efficiency, and innovation in road management.
